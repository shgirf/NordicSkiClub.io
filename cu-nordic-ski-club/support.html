Why Your Support Matters

The CU Nordic Ski Club relies on the generous support of alumni, parents, local businesses, and community members to provide high-quality skiing experiences for our members. Your contributions directly impact our ability to:

  • Subsidize membership dues for students with financial need
  • Purchase and maintain club equipment
  • Cover coaching and training expenses
  • Support travel to races and competitions
  • Organize group trips with reduced costs for members
  • Host clinics and educational events

Every donation, regardless of size, makes a meaningful difference in the lives of our student athletes and helps ensure that Nordic skiing remains accessible to all CU Boulder students.

Ways to Support

Financial Donations

Make a one-time or recurring donation to directly support club operations and student scholarships. All donations are tax-deductible.

Equipment Donations

Donate gently used or new skiing equipment. We accept skis, boots, poles, waxing supplies, and training equipment.

Business Sponsorship

Partner with us as a business sponsor. We offer various sponsorship levels with recognition opportunities.

Volunteer Your Time

Share your expertise by coaching, leading trips, helping with events, or providing professional services.

Alumni Network

Join our alumni network to stay connected, mentor current members, and support the next generation of Nordic skiers.

Spread the Word

Help us grow by sharing our story with your network, on social media, and within the skiing community.
