About CU Nordic Ski Club

Learn about our history, mission, and what makes our community special

Our Story

The CU Nordic Ski Club has been a cornerstone of the University of Colorado Boulder's outdoor community for over three decades. Founded by passionate skiers who wanted to share their love of cross-country skiing with fellow students, our club has grown into one of the most active and inclusive ski clubs in the region.

What began as a small group of enthusiasts has evolved into a thriving community that welcomes everyone from complete beginners to competitive athletes. We pride ourselves on maintaining the spirit of inclusivity and adventure that our founders envisioned.

Throughout our history, we've organized countless ski trips, hosted training sessions, competed in regional and national events, and most importantly, created lasting friendships and memories on the trails.

Our Mission & Values

Adventure

We embrace the spirit of adventure and exploration. From groomed trails to backcountry expeditions, we encourage our members to push their boundaries and discover new terrain.

Sustainability

We're committed to protecting the beautiful environments we ski in. Our club promotes responsible outdoor recreation and environmental stewardship.

Growth

We support personal growth and skill development. Through coaching, mentorship, and shared experiences, we help each member reach their skiing goals.

What We Offer

Regular Training Sessions

We organize weekly training sessions throughout the ski season, led by experienced coaches and club members. Sessions cater to all skill levels, from beginner technique clinics to advanced interval training.

Weekend Ski Trips

Explore Colorado's best Nordic ski areas with organized weekend trips. We handle logistics so you can focus on skiing and enjoying time with fellow club members.

Competitive Opportunities

For those interested in racing, we provide support for competing in USCSA events, regional competitions, and citizen races throughout the season.

Social Events

Beyond skiing, we host social gatherings, movie nights, equipment swaps, and end-of-season celebrations to keep our community connected year-round.
