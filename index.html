CU Boulder Nordic Ski Club

The CU Boulder Nordic Ski Club is a student organization at the University of Colorado Boulder dedicated to promoting cross-country skiing in a fun and inclusive environment. Whether you are new to the sport or a seasoned skier, the club provides opportunities for all skill levels to enjoy Nordic skiing, both recreationally and competitively.

Our Mission

At the CU Boulder Nordic Ski Club, we're building a powerful, inclusive space where every skier can thrive — from first-timers to seasoned racers.

We're here to:

  • Teach Nordic skiing (cross-country)
  • Elevate athletic potential — recreational or competitive
  • Create lasting connections and unforgettable experiences through organized trips, team events, and races

• We unite passionate individuals in a positive, driven, and supportive environment

• No experience? No problem. All are welcome.

A Community of Ski Enthusiasts

Whether you're aiming to race in USCSA-sanctioned events or just looking to ski for fun, the CU Boulder Nordic Ski Club offers an engaging, supportive environment where everyone feels welcome and included.
