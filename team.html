Interested in Leadership?

We hold elections for executive board positions each spring, and coordinator positions are filled on a rolling basis. Leadership experience in the club is a great way to develop skills, make connections, and shape the future of Nordic skiing at CU Boulder.

What we look for: Passion for Nordic skiing, commitment to inclusivity, strong communication skills, and dedication to serving the club community. Previous experience is helpful but not required!
